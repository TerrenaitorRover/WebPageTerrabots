About Us

Terrenaitor is a tech initiative born in 2024 in Peru, led by students, graduates, and professors from the National University of Engineering (UNI).

We design an autonomous rover for challenging environments, integrating engineering, science, and social vision to impact education, industry, and technological exploration. Our initiative seeks to combine academic excellence with practical experience, promoting innovation with a deep sense of social responsibility.

Terrabots is not just a team; it's a community of passionate minds who believe in the power of technology to transform realities. We work with perseverance and vision, driven by curiosity and collaboration to create sustainable, cutting-edge solutions that empower future generations.

Meet Our Team

Project History

In 2024, a group of students from the National University of Engineering (UNI) launched a project that combined technology, vision, and purpose—designing an autonomous rover capable of exploring extreme terrains with its own intelligence. What began as an idea in classrooms and labs soon became a multidisciplinary collaborative team, adding the talent of graduates and professors in mechatronics, electronics, systems, industrial engineering, and physics.

Through intense research, prototyping, and field testing, the team developed Terrenaitor a robust robot designed to navigate unpredictable terrain. We are on a trajectory toward national recognition, participation in international competitions such as the European Rover Challenge, and the formation of strong academic and industrial alliances.

Today, Terrenaitor stands as more than just a robot — it's a symbol of youth-led innovation, interdisciplinary synergy, and a vision of a smarter, more connected future for Peru and Latin America.
