European Rover Challenge 2025

TerrenaitorRover is going to participate in the European Rover Challenge 2025 (ERC), one of the most prestigious international competitions in terrestrial robotic exploration.

What is the ERC?
The European Rover Challenge (ERC) is an international space robotics competition that challenges university teams in Mars-like simulated environments. Tasks include autonomous navigation, object manipulation, terrain analysis, and AI-based decision-making.

Challenge: carry out simulated missions in Mars-like environments using an autonomous rover.

Location: Kielce, Poland

Read the complete event rules on the official ERC 2025 website.
