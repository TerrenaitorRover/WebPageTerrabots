What is Terrenaitor?

Terrenaitor is the autonomous rover being developed by the Terrabots team. It is an applied research project focused on the development of an autonomous ground rover. We integrate artificial intelligence, autonomous navigation systems, vision sensors, and advanced control technologies to simulate real-world exploration missions.

Team Divisions

Control

Responsible for the rover's navigation, sensors, motors, and steering system.

Artificial Intelligence

Develop algorithms for computer vision, autonomous navigation, and intelligent decision-making.

Mechanics & Electronics

Focus on chassis design, embedded electronics, and energy systems.
